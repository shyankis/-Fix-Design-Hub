My Story: From Struggle to Skill

Humble Beginnings

My journey started in a one-room house in Nagpur, facing immense financial hardships. These early challenges taught me the value of perseverance and hard work.

Discovering a Passion for Tech

While working at a mobile shop to survive, I discovered a passion for electronics. This hands-on experience was my real classroom, leading me to master mobile and laptop repair and complete several skill development courses.

Nalanda & A Mission to Serve

Joining Nalanda Lab was a life-changing experience. Under the guidance of incredible mentors, I found my purpose. Inspired by their mission, I helped establish over 50 community learning labs across India, bringing education to those who need it most.

Commitment to Lifelong Learning

My belief in education led me to complete my graduation and I am now pursuing an MA in Education. My journey is proof that it's never too late to learn and grow. Every skill I offer today is built on a foundation of real-world experience and relentless determination.
