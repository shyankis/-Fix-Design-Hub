My Journey: From Shadows to Light

The Echoes of Poverty

My story began in a small, humble room in Nagpur, where the daily struggle for survival was a constant companion. Financial hardships cast long shadows, but they also ignited an unyielding fire within me to rise above.

A Spark in the Darkness

Amidst working at a mobile shop to make ends meet, I found more than just a job; I discovered a profound passion for electronics. This hands-on experience became my true university, where I mastered mobile and laptop repair, completing several skill development courses that illuminated my path.

Nalanda: A Guiding Light

Joining Nalanda Lab was a turning point, a profound experience that redefined my life's purpose. Guided by incredible mentors, I embraced a mission beyond myself, contributing to the establishment of over 50 community learning labs across India, bringing the gift of education to countless deserving souls.

The Power of Unwavering Resolve

My unshakeable belief in education propelled me to complete my graduation, and I am now proudly pursuing an MA in Education. Every skill I offer, every repair I undertake, every design I create, is imbued with the spirit of that small room in Nagpur, fueled by real-world resilience and an unwavering commitment to growth. My journey is a testament to the boundless power of the human spirit.
